For me

My name is Ilina Shikerova and I travel the world for a living.
I’ve been shipwrecked in Indonesia. I’ve gone swimming in the frigid waters of Antarctica.
I’ve partied with Ernest Hemingway lookalikes in Key West, cheered on the world air guitar champions in Finland, and danced until dawn with fire-throwing Vikings in the Shetland Islands.
And I’ve traveled alone in places as far-flung as Lebanon, Colombia, and South Africa.
I’m a 29-year-old woman originally from the Boston area. After four years of establishing a career in online marketing and a lifetime dreaming of traveling the world, I decided that it was time to make it happen.
